General industry guidance per SMACNA HVAC Duct Construction Standards — always follow the project specification's stated class over this general reference.
Leakage Class formula (SMACNA): CL = (Q × 100) / (A × P^0.65), where Q = leakage rate (cfm), A = duct surface area (ft²), P = test static pressure (in. w.g.). This calculator converts your metric inputs internally and reports CL in its native (imperial) definition, since that's how leakage class is specified in tender documents and test certificates.
